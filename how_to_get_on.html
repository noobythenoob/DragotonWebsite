How to get on it -

  1. Go on google chrome - it only works on any Google search engine - NOT Bing, NOT Yahoo!
  2. Search - DragotonLogin.com/universal
  3. Type in username that you want - and password
  4. Pick which starter dragon you want
  5. Press create account. Your account will be processed to see if your username is appropriate, and wait for 5 minutes.
  6. Select your terrain, and start adventuring!
