Terrains

There are different terrains that correspond to the different elements of the dragons in the land of Dragoton. In each terrain, there is a chance to get:

  • dragons of that element
  • chests of that element
  • gems of that element
  • diferrent metals (for shopping) these metals can be combined with your dragon, and with enough pieces of metal, you can eventually become a METAL DRAGON! These are rare and use the pieces wisely!
  • weapons of that element
  • different arenas for each element can also be played!

However, there are a few exceptions to the terrains and arenas:

  • Magic terrains can only be accessed by magic portal
  • Space terrains can only be accessed with the space trident
  • Alliances can NOT come with you to magic OR space terrains - however, they CAN fight with you/against you in alliances
