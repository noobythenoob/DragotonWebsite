ELEMENTS

The types of regular elements (powers) are fire, water, ice, earth, rock, metal, wind, electricity, and nature.

The starter dragon is a regular dragon with no powers except physical powers such as clawing. If you start with anything else, please contact us, the developers, and we will get you any power you want, except the most powerful ones, light magic and dark magic!

There are also 5 different types of solar powers - which you can unlock in the game: Solar, Lunar, Starlight, Comet, and Deep Space.

Finally, there are different types of magics (besides light and dark) Pure, Soul, Chaos, Primal, and the most powerful, Legend.

Dragons can also come with 2 or more elements...
