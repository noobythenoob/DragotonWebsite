BATTLES

A battle in Dragoton takes LITERALLY 5 minutes. Here's how it works:

  • only 8 dragons per battle
  • each one of your dragons have moves based on your elements!
  • when your dragons get powerful enough, you can unlock golden moves: the most powerful of them of all
  • if no one is defeated over these 5 minutes, SUDDEN DEATH will appear on your screen, and you'll have 1 minute to get rid of it.. and of course, if you don't, SUDDEN DEATH will happen... and there's nothing you can do to stop it.... MWAHAHAHAHAH!

    • There are 4 types of battles:

    1. Alliance Battles
    2. Arena Battles
    3. Conquest Battles
    4. Dragon Cup

      5. Heres what each battle means:

  • Alliance Battles: you battle with someone in your alliance - Your teammates - and if you beat them, you get an ALLIANCE CHEST! They can have lots of valuable things!!!
  • Arena Battles: You battle with someone randomly selected from arround the world, and when you win, you can get prize boxes!
  • Conquest Battles: your WHOLE alliance battles against another alliance... each person chooses 1 AND ONLY 1 one dragon per member... if your alliance wins, you get alliance points!!! (which determines how good an alliance is)
  • Dragon Cup Battles: these battles can let you win a dragon once you complete the cup! Each cup will have from 5 - 13 stages, but sadly, there are only 20 cup battles avalable currently...
